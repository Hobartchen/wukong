About

Miners Gold Brewery is a restaurant and taproom located in the mining town of Beaconsfield in Tasmania's Tamar Valley.

We offer brewery tours and tastings, as well as a range of fresh beer and local food.

you can find us right next to the Beaconsifield Mine and Heritage Centre.
